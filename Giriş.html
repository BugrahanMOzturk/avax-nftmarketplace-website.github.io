What can you do on our marketplace?

Create nft

You can easily create and mint your NFTs as you wish. Likewise, you can create Collections and create special NFTs for them.

CHECK OUT NFTS ON THE MARKET

You can browse the NFTs produced and owned by other users or creators in the market; You can sell them or reach their sellers' profiles and negotiate

SEE YOUR NFT'S

You can access all the NFTs you own at once and access their information.

browse for NFTs created by users

You can take a look at the NFTs created by creators and make an offer to the creator for other content of the creators you like. If the NFT you are interested in is listed, you can buy it directly.

buy nft

You can access an NFT in the market, see the NFT's information, seller, contract address and buy it with a single click for the current price.

list nft

You can list the NFTs you own for any price you want and make them visible to other users in the market. You can make a profit in the AXAV network with low fees and low commissions of our site.

browse top creators

You can see and list the artists who create NFTs on our website. You can follow the creators who produce the most NFTs and sell the most

browse leaderboard

You can see the users with the highest trade value on our site, browse their profiles and see how much AVAX they spent on the site.

see your profile

You can view and update your information by visiting your profile. There is an e-mail area for them to contact you. You can also see the number of NFTs you own and look at your spending amount.
